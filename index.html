The Artist

More Than a
Songwriter

Christofe Lain is a published recording artist, ASCAP-registered songwriter, and author operating out of Nashville, TN, the heartbeat of American music. Originally from Yonkers, New York, Christofe brings a raw, street-level authenticity to every song he touches.

Under the Sly Doggie Productions and Liberator MUSIC banner, Christofe has spent decades honing a craft that bridges genre lines, combining soulful lyricism with melodic songwriting that resonates across audiences. His self-described style: "Can play and write like crazy."

  • ASCAP Registered Songwriter & Publishing Rights Holder
  • Recording Artist under Sly Doggie Prod. / CMG Nashville
  • Author with published works alongside his music catalog
  • Mercy University alumnus bringing academic depth to artistry
  • Collaborative artist, open to sync licensing & co-writes

The Journey

A Life Lived
in Music

From the streets of Yonkers to the studios of Nashville. Christofe Lain's story is one of relentless passion, talent, and an unshakeable belief in the power of a great song.

Yonkers, NY
The Beginning
Raised in Yonkers, New York, a city that shaped his voice and gave him the grit to tell real stories through music. The love affair with songwriting started early and never let go.
Mercy University
Academic Foundation
Christofe pursued formal education at Mercy University, where he deepened his understanding of storytelling, writing, and the craft behind great music and literature.
Liberator MUSIC
ASCAP Registration
Registered with ASCAP under Liberator MUSIC, Christofe locked in his publishing rights and established himself as a serious professional in the music industry.
Sly Doggie Prod.
Nashville Recording
Partnered with Sly Doggie Productions and CMG Nashville to record and release a catalog of original music, including standout Spotify tracks like "Stay With You" and "Relive."
Now
Publishing & Beyond
A published recording artist, songwriter, and author. Christofe continues creating, collaborating, and building a legacy one song at a time.
